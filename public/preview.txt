APTM WEBSITE PREVIEW
===================

Modern, responsive business website for:
✓ Associação Portuguesa de Terapia da Mão
✓ Next.js 16 + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS
✓ Professional blue/gray color scheme

✓ Complete pages: Home, About, Services, Events, Trainings, Contact

✓ SEO optimized and accessible

- Event calendar placeholder
- Training program information
- Contact form with validation
- Team member profiles
- Timeline/history sections
- FAQ sections
- Professional footer with social links

Ready for development and deployment!
